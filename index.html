🔎 Where is my game.sii?

  1. Open C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\userdata\<YourSteamID>\227300\remote\profiles\
  2. Inside, there's one folder per profile (hex-encoded name). Open it.
  3. Go into save\ and pick the slot you want (e.g. autosave or a numbered one).
  4. Inside is game.sii. Drag it onto the box above. Done. 🚛
🔒 Privacy: Your save never leaves your browser. All parsing, decryption, and analysis happens locally on your device. This site doesn't upload, log, or store anything. Check the source code.
